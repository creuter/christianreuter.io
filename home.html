Hello, I'm Christian!

Im a digital product designer living in Raleigh, NC with my wife, kid, and dog. Ive designed interfaces for the past ~13 years.

Currently, Im working remotely as a lead product designer at Iterable, a growth marketing platform based in San Francisco that lets growth practitioners spend more time on creative and compassionate pursuits.

Previously, I was the design director at thoughtbots Raleigh studio, where we built custom web and mobile applications for our clients around the Southeast.

In my spare time Im chipping away at Pickup, an app to help school administrators handle the chaos of carpooling and keep their students safe. Or, for non-designery stuff, hanging out with my wife and son, playing Sudoku, and learning woodworking in my garage. (Can you tell I wrote this during the pandemic?)

Im a broad generalist, currently most interested in scaling quality and culture of high-functioning, distributed design teams.

I love connecting with designers and other makers. If youre in the area, working on an interesting project, or have thoughts on remote design work shoot me an email! Id love to chat.
